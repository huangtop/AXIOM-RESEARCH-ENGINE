# Industry Graph v0.5

The graph models causal industry dependencies through canonical entities.

Example path:

SK hynix → HBM4 → Vera Rubin → NVIDIA

Parallel capacity path:

TSMC → CoWoS → Vera Rubin → NVIDIA

Edges carry type, strength, confidence, optional lead/lag, and evidence references. Exposures connect a company to supply, capacity, and demand dependencies and may reference research drivers.
